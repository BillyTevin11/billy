MONITORING THE EFFECT OF ALGAL BLOOMS ON THE LESSER FLAMINGO HABITAT IN LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL PARK

The project aims to monitor the impact of algal blooms on the habitat of lesser flamingos in Lake Nakuru, a renowned alkaline lake located in Kenya. Algal blooms can significantly affect the ecological balance of the lake, potentially disrupting the food chain and threatening the survival of lesser flamingos, which heavily rely on the lake's unique ecosystem. By studying the relationship between algal blooms and flamingo habitat, this project seeks to provide valuable insights for conservation efforts and the sustainable management of Lake Nakuru.
